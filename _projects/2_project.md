My mom, dad and I went to London for the first time. I absolutely loved it. My parents don't travel anywhere, but luckily they have me as their unabashed, adventurous daughter (what would they do without me, really) to parade them around the whole city and get an ungodly amount of steps in. I loved how historic London was--I felt like a citizen of the world--and everyone knows I love dressing up, so posh was on the menu everyday.
